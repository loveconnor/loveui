The **LoveUI** community is built on kindness, collaboration, and respect.  
Whether you’re fixing a bug, improving documentation, or suggesting a new feature — you’re part of the team.

---

## Be Respectful

We follow a [Code of Conduct](https://github.com/loveconnor/loveui/blob/main/.github/CODE_OF_CONDUCT.md).  
Everyone is expected to keep discussions **friendly, inclusive, and constructive**.  
Remember: every contributor — including maintainers — is giving their time and energy to make this project better.

When giving feedback, be thoughtful.  
When receiving feedback, assume good intent.  
We’re all here to learn and build something great together.

---

## Ask Questions

If you’re unsure how to do something, **just ask**.  
There’s no such thing as a bad question.  
It’s always better to get clarification than to spend time heading in the wrong direction.

You can:
- Open a discussion on GitHub  
- Join community threads  
- Ask for guidance before submitting a PR  

We’re happy to help you get started.

---

## Thank You

Every contribution — big or small — helps move LoveUI forward.  
By sharing your time, ideas, and code, you’re helping developers around the world build better interfaces faster.

We’re grateful for your support and can’t wait to see what you create.  
**Thank you for being part of LoveUI — and happy coding!**
